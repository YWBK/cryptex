📜 Quick Rules

The Setup

One person is the GM ("Everyone Else"). All others are Voices in John's head — John is an insane man in Minneapolis. Each Voice has: Willpower, 2–3 Skills, and a secret Obsession (level 1–3). Voices start at 10 WP (2 skills) or 7 WP (3 skills).

Rolling for Success

Roll 1d6. Need 3+ with a relevant Skill, or a 6 without one. Before rolling, spend any number of Willpower for +1 per point.

Bidding for Control

A test happens when John wakes up, gets hurt, or the active Voice fails or completes their obsession:

  • All interested Voices secretly and simultaneously bid Willpower.
  • Highest bidder takes control and loses their bid amount.
  • All others keep their bids.
  • On a tie → roll off (each tied player rolls 1d6, highest wins).

John Sleeps

If nothing exciting for ~10 minutes, GM rolls d6. On 4+ John naps → all Voices gain 1 WP → new test for control when he wakes.

Winning

Game ends when all Voices hit 0 WP. Reveal obsessions. Score = completions × obsession level. Highest score wins — and is usually GM next game!
